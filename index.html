Alexandra Kollontai

Alexandra Kollontai was the first woman cabinet member in world history, and implemented a series of comprehensive reforms that uplifted the condition of children and working women in Soviet Russia. Her socialist-feminist ideas continue to shape our understanding of the condition of women under the capitalist system today.

Angela Davis

Angela Davis is a political activist, academic, and author, who has been highly involved in the civil rights movement in the U.S. She is well known for her work and influence on racial justice, women's rights, and criminal justice reform. Davis is a professor emerita at the University of California, Santa Cruz, in its History of Consciousness Department, and a former director of the university's Feminist Studies Department.
